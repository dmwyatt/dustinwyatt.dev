Dustin Wyatt

Hi, my name is Dustin. I live in Desloge, MO, USA which is a small town near St. Louis. I'm married with a 9-year-old daughter. I play video games, run, and talk about philosophy and AI on the internet.

I started learning to write software on a Tandy "CoCo" TRS-80 when I was just a kid by typing BASIC games in from computer magazines. I've been doing it for fun and professionally ever since.

In the early 2000s I had a detour into home-building and I ran my own owner-operated construction company (and swung a hammer on the job site!). It was here that I best learned how to manage time, manage people, and make clients happy.

Since that time I've been writing and designing software. I'm a passionate learner and always interested in learning about new technologies, while at the same time recognizing the value of tried-and-true solutions.

Lately I've been experimenting with machine learning and computer vision for a variety of tasks. Experiments I'm working on currently:

  • Training models to see what kind of information we can extract from screenshots of people's computers.
  • Using Raspberry Pi cameras to do presence detection of specific people in specific rooms for my home automation system.
